Cosmo is a chat mod for Canvas Mod Loader. It overlays as an alternative method of chatting with mod users

---

## Core Features
- **Global-chat**: Connect and talk in real-time with other players across the world.
- **Permanent Nicknames**: Your nickname is permanently bound to your Sky-ID to prevent impersonation. If you reinstall canvas, remove the mod, or even try to tamper with the files, your original nickname will automatically restore when you connect.
- **Local Time Formatting**: Timestamps automatically convert and display in your device's local timezone.
- **Crown Badge**: Some users might have a crown next to their name. It's manually given.
- **Moderation**: Click on any username in the chat to file a report. Abuse, spam, extreme toxicity, selling, and extreme NSFW activities are strictly prohibited and subject to bans (you can say a lot of stuff, just use common sense).
- **Messages get deleted every 24 hours**
---

## Installation

To install the mod on your device:

1. Download `libcosmo.so` from the [Releases Page](https://github.com/MisterGatto/Cosmo/releases/latest).
2. Add `libcosmo.so` file in Canvas by clicking on `ADD MOD`.
3. Tap on one of the launch buttons to launch the game.
